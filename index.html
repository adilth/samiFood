about

discover our Restaurant Story

we samiFood is restaurant drawing inspiration from all across the south and the world. We don't like to get boxed into a specific regional style as we love it all. In addition to the fan favorites of ribs, wings, brisket and sausage, we specialize in creative sandwiches using all of our marrakesh meats with flavorful sauces and condiments.

samiFood is run by a small, passionate and hardworking team, most of whom were born and raised in the MR area. We work hard every day to provide the best food, service and experience we can while making sure our menu stays at a price point where you can be a once or twice-a-week regular.
